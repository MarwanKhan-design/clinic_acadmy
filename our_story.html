Our Story

Dedicated to Your Health & Wellbeing

Founded in 2002 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, Wellness Medical Clinic began with a simple mission: to provide personalized, patient-centered care in a warm and welcoming environment. What started as a small family practice has grown into a multispecialty clinic serving thousands of patients each year.

Today, we're proud to have a team of 15+ board-certified physicians, 30+ nursing staff, and state-of-the-art facilities—all while maintaining the personal touch that has always defined us. We believe that healthcare isn't just about treating illness; it's about building lasting relationships and empowering our patients to live their healthiest lives.
