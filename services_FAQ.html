Frequently Asked Questions

About our services

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

+

It depends on your insurance plan. Some plans require a referral from your primary care physician, while others allow direct access. Our staff can help you understand your coverage.

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

You can schedule online through our website, call our office, or use the patient portal. New patients are welcome!

What should I bring to my first appointment?

+

Please bring your ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results.
