Our Journey

The Wellness Medical Clinic Story

Two decades of growth, innovation, and compassionate care

2002

Clinic Founded

Dr. Sarah Mitchell opens a small family practice with just two exam rooms and a vision for personalized care.

2008

Expansion to Multi-Specialty

Added cardiology and dermatology services, growing to 5 physicians and 15 support staff.

2012

New Facility Opening

Moved to our current 25,000 sq. ft. facility with state-of-the-art equipment and expanded services.

2015

Patient Portal Launch

Introduced digital health records and online appointment scheduling for patient convenience.

2018

Telehealth Services

Launched virtual consultations, making healthcare more accessible than ever.

2023

Patient Safety Excellence Award

Recognized for outstanding patient safety and quality of care.
