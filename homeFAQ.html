Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our clinic and services

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

You can schedule online through our website, call us at (555) 123-4567, or use our patient portal. New patients are always welcome!

What insurance plans do you accept?

+

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Medicare, and Medicaid. Contact us to verify your coverage.

Do you offer virtual visits?

+

Yes, we offer telehealth appointments for many types of consultations. It's convenient, secure, and covered by most insurance plans.

What should I bring to my first visit?

+

Please bring your ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous providers.

How do I get my test results?

+

Results are typically available within 48-72 hours through our secure patient portal. You'll also receive a call from our office for critical results.

Do you accept walk-ins?

+

Yes, we accept walk-ins for our urgent care services. However, appointments are recommended for routine and specialty care to minimize wait times.
